About Us

We have dedicated team of professionals committed to designing excellence and offering comprehensive service in Interior Designing and Project Consultation. In this firm the Architects & the Interior Designers work in close collaboration with other experts including Engineers, Graphic Designers, Artists, Landscape Designers, Lighting & Acoustic Specialists.

Client is the soul of the project. Sakshi nagar designs has seen a constant steady growth. The core purpose of the company is to create spaces that inspire, and turn dreams into reality. With an uncompromising attitude towards quality and constant up-gradation of technology and trends, the firm strives to transform spaces and lives.

Our Services

Sakshi nagar & interior designers is a full-service interior designer and decorator that specializes in commercial, residential and office spaces for decades.

Residential Design

Residential design is the design of the interior of private residences (like Home). As this type design is very specific for individual situations, the needs and wants of the individual are paramount in this area of interior design. The interior designer may work on the project from the initial planning stage or may work on the remodelling of an existing structure. It is often a very involved process that takes months to fine-tune and create a space with the vision of the client.

Office Design

A look inside the offices of some of the world's leading companies and creative businesses, with the best office interior architecture and design. Office interior including every area of that like reception area, conference room , work place , Meeting room etc.

Commercial Design

Commercial design encompasses a wide range of subspecialties like Retail (includes malls, shopping centers, department stores and showrooms), Corporate (office design for any kind of business), Healthcare ( Hospitals, medical offices, dentist offices and laboratories),Hospitality and recreation (Includes hotels, motels, resorts, cafes, bars, casinos, nightclubs, theaters, music and concert halls, opera houses, sports venues, restaurants, gyms, health clubs and spas etc) ,Institutional etc.
